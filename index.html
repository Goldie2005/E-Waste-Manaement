Project Objectives

  • To create awareness about the harmful effects of improper e-waste disposal
  • To educate users on safe and eco-friendly e-waste recycling methods
  • To design a user-friendly web platform using modern web technologies
  • To promote responsible use and disposal of electronic devices
  • To provide information about collection, segregation, and recycling of e-waste
  • To develop a scalable system that can be extended with backend and database support
